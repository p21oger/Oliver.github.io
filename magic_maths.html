Magic Maths

Trick 1

  1. Multiply shoe size (round to whole number) by 5
  2. Add 50
  3. Multiply by 20
  4. Add the year divided by 2
  5. Add the year you're in minus 1000
  6. Subtract the year you're born
  7. FINISHED!! The first digit is your shoe size and the last 2 are you age

Trick 2

  1. Think of a whole number from 1 through 10
  2. Double it
  3. Add 4
  4. Divide by 2
  5. Subtract the original number
  6. FINISHED!! Is the numeral 2?!

Trick 3

  1. Choose a whole number, any number
  2. Multiply it by 100
  3. Subtract the original number from the answer
  4. Add the digits in your answer
  5. FINISHED!! Is you number 18?!

Trick 4

  1. Think of a number
  2. Multiply your number by 3
  3. Add 45
  4. Double it
  5. Divide the answer by 6
  6. Take away the number you first thought of
  7. FINISHED!! The answer is 15!
